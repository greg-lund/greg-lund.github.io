About this project

As a Research Assistant in the Automated Robotics and Perception Group's lab, I work to support a team competing in this years DARPA Subterranean challenge. The first project I have been working on is to design and fabricate a system to deploy radio systems with extending antennas on an autonomous robot for use in the challenge.
