About this project

The goal of this project is to demonstrate the ability to sequence a passive solar array deployment. Our payload will be launched on a rocket from Wallops Flight Facility, to an elevation of around 150 km. At apogee we will deploy our panel stack on a linear rail system and the sequencing will begin. The panels will deploy one at a time, using only strain energy from the tape springs(currently cut up tape measures).
